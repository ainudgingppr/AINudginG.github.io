研究團隊 / Research Team

香港都會大學研究團隊 Hong Kong Metropolitan University Research Team

本研究由香港都會大學李兆基商業管理學院主導，聚焦人工智能助推在香港旅遊及酒店業可持續消費中的應用、成效與倫理邊界。 This project is led by the Lee Shau Kee School of Business Administration at Hong Kong Metropolitan University, with a focus on AI nudging, sustainable consumption, and responsible tourism and hospitality practice.

主導院校 / Lead Institution

香港都會大學 / Hong Kong Metropolitan University

李兆基商業管理學院 / Lee Shau Kee School of Business Administration

項目負責人 / Principal Investigator

Huimin Liu / 劉慧敏

香港都會大學李兆基商業管理學院 / Lee Shau Kee School of Business Administration, Hong Kong Metropolitan University

劉慧敏為香港都會大學李兆基商業管理學院助理教授，專研旅遊及酒店管理。她的研究興趣包括旅客體驗、線上與線下旅客行為，以及旅遊情境中的創傷經驗。

Huimin Liu is an Assistant Professor at Lee Shau Kee School of Business and Administration, Hong Kong Metropolitan University, specializing in tourism and hospitality. Her research interests include tourist experience, online and offline tourist behavior, and trauma in tourism contexts.

研究助理 / Research Assistant

Aaron Chung / 鍾承熙

Aaron Chung 是行為經濟學研究人員，研究人們如何作出決策，以及行為改變為何成功或失敗。他的研究興趣涵蓋助推、選擇架構，以及能協助人們在日常與政策情境中作出更佳選擇的干預設計。他的工作運用行為科學框架及因果推論、實驗設計等實證方法。

Aaron Chung is a behavioural economic researcher whose research examines how people make decisions and what makes behaviour change succeed or fail. His interests span nudging, choice architecture, and the design of interventions that help people make better choices across a range of everyday and policy contexts. His work applies behavioural science frameworks and empirical methods like causal inference and experimental designs.
